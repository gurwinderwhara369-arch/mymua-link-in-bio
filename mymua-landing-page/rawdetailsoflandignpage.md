# One concern I have

The phrase **"10 templates"** can actually work against you. Visitors may think the library is small.

Instead, emphasize growth:

> **Start with 10 professionally designed templates, with 2 new designs added every week.**

That changes the perception from "only 10" to "constantly expanding."

---

# My biggest recommendation

Your landing page should not feel like a SaaS dashboard.

It should feel like a **luxury beauty brand**.

Imagine if **Apple**, **Dior Beauty**, and **Linear** collaborated on a website builder for makeup artists.

That means:

* Elegant typography instead of oversized marketing fonts.
* Refined spacing with generous whitespace.
* Smooth, understated motion rather than flashy effects.
* Photography and templates that look editorial.
* Every animation serving a purpose: showing transformation, building trust, or guiding attention.

If someone lands on your homepage and thinks, **"This feels like the kind of premium brand I want my own business to look like,"** you've already won half the battle before they even click **Start for ₹599**.
